About Acropolis

What is Acropolis?

Acropolis is a sustainability tool designed to see where your building matches up to similar buildings. Metrics that we use to determine our rankings are EnergySTAR Score, Natural Gas usage, Electricity usage, Water usage, and building carbon emissions.


Where does the data come from?

All Data Acropolis uses is open data from New York City's website. The data is published annually and is required by law to be collected and published


How can I use Acropolis?

Right now, our website has two primary features. You can look up a building on the 'Lookup tab' and see it how it matches up compared to others, or you can just see an absolute ranking of buildings on the 'Rankings' tab.
